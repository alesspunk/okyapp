Tokens

Color and semantic tokens

  • --primary-main #410088, --primary-light #bc9edc, --primary-dark #230741
  • --secondary-main #18afa5, --secondary-light #8adad7, --secondary-dark #057a73
  • Semantic sets: --success-*, --warning-*, --error-*, --info-*

Recent surface and effect tokens

  • --back-drop: scrim / modal backdrop surface
  • --card-top: top gradient surface with 24px radius and top-side border treatment
  • --card-middle: radial background used by Middle Card
  • --card-bottom: bottom gradient surface with 16px radius and bottom-side border treatment
  • --separator-border: dashed border token for separators and guide wrappers
  • --card-shadow: reusable card shadow token
  • --pdp-card-shadow: PDP shell effect token
  • --border-main: neutral border used by card-like shells

Reference

Character recommendations

  • Middle Card / Vale de Monto: title max 14 chars, amount max 5 digits + separators
  • Middle Card / eGift Card: title max 21 chars
  • Toast Banners: max 30 chars recommended
  • Brand Item label: one line only, auto-centered
  • Folder: use country abbreviations already defined by the component
